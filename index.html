Hi, I am Gunvir Dhesi and I am currently a Grade 10 student at Chingouscy Secondary School. Since a young age I have been fasinated with computers and electroics in general. In Grade 4/5 I was introduced to coding becuase of a game called Agar.io. I started using a chrome extension "Temper Monkey" to start making small game changes, little did I know this was going to be the beggining of something big. In Grade 6 I attended a regional program(go to education for more info) which gave me the chance to work with a lot more computers, I learnt about how computers function and how the operatiing/bios work. Soon after with the help of my Grade 6 teacher my class and I were going around our school to download and educate students about linux OS.
